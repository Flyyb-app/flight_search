FLYYB Terms & Conditions

1. Booking & Payments

All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation. Prices displayed include taxes and fees unless stated otherwise. Payment is processed securely via Stripe using 256-bit SSL encryption. FLYYB reserves the right to cancel bookings where payment cannot be verified. Full payment is required at time of booking.

2. Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure are eligible for a full refund to the original payment method within 5-10 business days. Cancellations within 24 hours of departure are non-refundable. FLYYB credits earned on a cancelled booking will be reversed from your account.

3. Rescheduling

Bookings may be rescheduled up to 48 hours before the scheduled departure. Rescheduling is subject to availability on the new date. Any price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly. Only one reschedule per booking is permitted.

4. FLYYB Credits Programme

You earn 5% of your booking total as FLYYB credits upon confirmed payment. Credits may be applied to future bookings up to a maximum of 20% of the total fare. Credits have no cash value and are non-transferable. Credits associated with cancelled bookings will be reversed. FLYYB reserves the right to modify or discontinue the credits programme with 30 days notice.

5. AIRA — AI Concierge

AIRA is an AI-powered assistant designed to help you search and book flights. AIRA uses Google Gemini AI and may occasionally produce inaccurate results — always verify flight details before completing payment. Conversations with AIRA are processed by Google and subject to Google's privacy policy. AIRA does not store your payment information. Final booking terms are governed by these Terms & Conditions, not AIRA's responses.

6. Baggage Policy

Economy class: 23kg checked baggage + 7kg cabin baggage. Business class: 32kg checked baggage + 10kg cabin baggage. First class: 40kg checked + 10kg cabin. Excess baggage charges apply at check-in. FLYYB is not responsible for items lost or damaged during transit.

7. Passenger Responsibility

Passengers are responsible for ensuring valid travel documents including passport, visa and health requirements for their destination. FLYYB is not liable for denied boarding due to insufficient documentation. Passenger details provided at booking must exactly match travel documents.

8. Privacy

Your personal data is collected and stored securely to process bookings and provide our service. We do not sell your data to third parties. Data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws. For full details see our Privacy Policy at flyyb.vercel.app.

9. Liability

FLYYB acts as an intermediary between passengers and airlines. We are not liable for flight delays, cancellations or disruptions caused by airlines or circumstances beyond our control. Our maximum liability is limited to the value of the booking.

10. Governing Law

These terms are governed by applicable law. By creating an account and making bookings you agree to these Terms & Conditions in full. FLYYB reserves the right to update these terms at any time.
